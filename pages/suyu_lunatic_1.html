Chapter 1: Introduction

Suyu Lunatic First Edition is a Trackmania event hosted by Suyu Salu, held on June 27, 2025. Players competed on 10 custom lunatic-style maps, all built by Suyu himself. This page covers the results, highlights and information about the event.

As stated in the trailer video, if a player finishes all the 10 maps they recieve the beautiful suyu_salu skin.
